Derived feature stack

Every input is resampled to a common 30 m grid, then reduced to per-pixel drivers the model reasons over.

🌿 Surface & vegetation

  • NDVI / green fraction
  • Impervious surface fraction
  • Albedo
  • LULC class

🏙️ Urban morphology

  • Building density & height
  • Sky-view factor
  • Street-canyon geometry
  • Distance to water

🌤️ Atmosphere

  • Air temperature
  • Relative humidity
  • Wind speed
  • Time of day / season

Tech stack

Google Earth Engine Python · rasterio · xarray GeoPandas PyTorch (PINN) XGBoost / LightGBM SHAP SciPy optimize SOLWEIG / InVEST Leaflet / deck.gl dashboard
Optional physical models — SOLWEIG (mean radiant temperature at street level) and InVEST Urban Cooling — serve as independent physical baselines to cross-validate the ML model's cooling estimates, strengthening trust in the results.
